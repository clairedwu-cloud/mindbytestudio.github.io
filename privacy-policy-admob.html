App: Arrow Mind  ·  Developer: MindByte Studio  ·  Last updated: March 2026

MindByte Studio ("we", "us", or "our") built Arrow Mind as a free app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our app. By using Arrow Mind, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Arrow Mind itself does not directly collect personal information. However, the app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect the following data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID / IDFA)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device type, operating system, and language
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
  • App usage data and diagnostics
Local data only: Your game progress (completed levels) is saved locally on your device and never sent to any server.

2. How We Use Information

The data collected via AdMob is used to:

  • Display relevant advertisements within the app

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for any purpose beyond operating and improving the app experience.

We use Google AdMob, a service provided by Google LLC, to serve ads in our app. AdMob may use cookies, device identifiers, and similar tracking technologies. Google's data collection and use is governed by Google's own Privacy Policy.

  • Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • How Google uses data from apps: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
  • Opt out of personalized ads: adssettings.google.com

4. Advertising ID & Your Choices

AdMob uses your device's Advertising ID to serve personalized ads. You can reset or opt out of interest-based advertising at any time:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID (Android 12+) or opt out of personalized ads
  • You can also opt out at adssettings.google.com
Note for EEA/UK users: If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, AdMob will request your consent before serving personalized ads, in compliance with GDPR and UK GDPR. You may withdraw consent at any time via the in-app consent dialog.

5. Data Retention

Game progress data is stored locally on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data. AdMob data retention is governed by Google's own retention policies.
